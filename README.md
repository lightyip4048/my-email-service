## Our association
My company is an alumni association for a Christian band (called Principle De Unique Christian Band, 源理基督徒樂隊) in Hong Kong

## Our Email Usage
We request AWS SES production access exclusively for:

1. **Event Promotions**  
   Sending announcements about alumni gatherings, reunion concerts, and worship sessions to our registered members.

2. **Community Connections**  
   Sharing newsletters, prayer requests, and ministry updates with members who have explicitly subscribed to our communications.

3. **Organizational Updates**  
   Notifying members about association news, leadership changes, and volunteer opportunities.

## Key Commitments

| Commitment | Implementation |
|------------|----------------|
| **Permission-Only Sending** | We will ONLY send emails to members who have provided their email addresses voluntarily through our registration process. |
| **Clear Unsubscribe Option** | Every email will include a visible `List-Unsubscribe` header and an unsubscribe link. |
| **Low Volume** | Estimated 500-2,000 emails per month, primarily during event seasons. |
| **Complaint Handling** | We will immediately remove any member who marks our email as spam from our mailing list. |
| **No Purchased Lists** | We will NEVER buy, rent, or trade email lists. |
